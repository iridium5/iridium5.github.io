What is Metrecycle?

Metrecycle is an Electronic Waste collection, refurbishing & disassembly service. We set up work sites in developing areas, and then collect E-Waste from surrounding businesses, communities and landfills. We then disassemble and sort the E-Waste into categories that can be sold downstream to Recycling facilities and interested buyers. For electronics that are still in decent shape, we refurbish/reuse them so that they can be sold to surrounding communities for a fair price. Our sites will provide jobs for workers with limited skill-sets that otherwise would have difficulty finding employment.

How it Works

Collect E-Waste

Metrecycle collects Electronics via large OEM/ODM's, corporations and also consumer drop-offs. By collecting E-Waste directly, we prevent it from entering the hands of groups that exhibit poor practices such as dumping in landfills.

Train and employ workers

Metrecycle hires workers from the surrounding communities. We train them to disassemble electronics & also run quality checks on electronics that are still operational. The quality electronics are refurbished/resold. The reamining electronics are disassembled & separated into waste streams.

Sell refurbished electronics & commodities

With the electronics that we are able to refurbish, we sell them in developing countries for a fair price, empowering local citizens that otherwise couldn't afford them. The disassembled electronics are separated into commodity categories which are sold to downstream buyers like recycling facilities.

  • A Novel Solution

    Currently the industry is populated with Government programs and NGO's that are extremely inefficient, and lack innovation.

    Metrecycle is breaking the mold by taking a lean and innovative approach to the table, which will allow for more efficiency and progress.

    One example of our innovation is the Metrecycle 'pop-up' work sites concept. Rather than spending hundreds of thousands of dollars to build large factories, we are retrofitting shipping containers to turn them into work spaces. Our sites take up a small footprint and make our business more mobile and scalable.
